# General Hotel Information

## Overview of The Grand Hotel

The Grand Hotel and the iconic stone archway at the entrance to the old town of Buzet serve as significant historical landmarks. For centuries, they have welcomed travelers, remaining open to guests from all walks of life. Located in the heart of the old town, among cobblestone streets, the family-run Grand Hotel embodies the town's rich heritage.

## Restaurant and Loggia

### Istrian "Wine & Dine" Experience

At the Grand Hotel restaurant, guests can indulge in an authentic Istrian culinary journey. Featuring local ingredients and innovative interpretations by the chef, this is the perfect opportunity to immerse yourself in the region’s rich gastronomic culture.

---

### Seasonal Istrian Cuisine

- Boškarin (Istrian ox)  
- Asparagus, truffles, and mushrooms  
- Locally made cheese, prosciutto, and olive oil

Every dish is crafted to represent the flavors and aromas of Istria.

---

### Philosophy of Freshness

The chef’s kitchen is committed to seasonal, local ingredients, selected daily. Great attention is paid to presentation, creating a visual and culinary masterpiece.

---

### Wine Pairing and Cellar

Istrian food pairs beautifully with its renowned wines:

- **Malvazija**: The queen of Istrian white wines  
- **Teran**: A robust red wine typical of the region

Visit the wine cellar for a wider selection of premium wines.
